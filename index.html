Hello There!

Hey! I’m a game designer focused on polish, game feel, and making gameplay that’s not just functional, but fun and satisfying.

I’ve developed both 2D arcade-style indie games and full 3D projects, often working solo across design, programming, and visual assets. Beyond games, I’ve also created interactive educational experiences for museums and institutions.

I enjoy prototyping and fine-tuning gameplay until every movement, hit, and transition feels right. I work with tools like Unity, Photoshop, Aseprite, Illustrator, and Crocotile 3D to bring my ideas to life from concept to final polish.

My design style is inspired by the exploration and storytelling of Tunic, Hollow Knight, and Dark Souls, combined with the fast-paced, fluid gameplay of Devil May Cry, Downwell, or Smash Bros. I aim to create experiences that feel good to control and even better to master.
